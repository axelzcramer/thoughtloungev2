OK... SO WHAT'S A THOUGHT LOUNGE?

A thought lounge is a formatted dialogue in which 4-6 participants each bring 1 topic, which is of utmost interest to him/her and which s/he thinks needs to be talked about, to a designated place. Each participant is given 12 minutes to facilitate a dialogue on the topic (that means you have the podium for 12 minutes. You can make it a full out presentation (powerpoint and all), facilitate an activity, or just write something up on the board and let the dialogue run.) After each topic is given its time, all topics are written up on the blackboard and 12 minutes are given to connect them all.
